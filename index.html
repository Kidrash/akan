Ghanaian Akan Names

We all have names , but not every one knows how or they were named. However there are some people who know where ther names origianated from. These are people from Ghana, a west african country. Ghanaians have a naming system where they get named depending on the day they are born.

*HOW IT WORKS*

By providing your details as required below the application will take your birth dates and calculate the day of week in which you were born hence giving you your Akan name.
